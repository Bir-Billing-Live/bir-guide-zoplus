Chokling Monastery
1.2km

Nestled in the Tibetan Colony, Chokling Monastery in Bir offers tranquil surroundings and a unique atmosphere. This monastery, with its white exterior adorned with bright colors, provides peaceful views of the mountains. It's conveniently located just 1 km from Bir Landing Site, allowing visitors to enjoy paragliders in the sky. The serene setting, meditation center lawns, and fluttering colorful flags make Chokling Monastery a must-visit spot in Bir Billing.

Palpung Sherabling

Nestled within the Bhatu forest at the foothills of the Himalayas, Sherabling Monastery spans 3 acres of tranquil land. Along the pathway, pine trees line both sides, while colorful prayer flags offer a warm welcome. The monastery's main prayer hall houses a stunning two-story golden Maitreya statue, and its expansive complex includes beautiful Buddhist temples, residential complex, and stupas. Inside, the chants of Buddhist monks immersed in prayer create a deeply spiritual ambiance.

The Dzongsar Khyentse Institute is a Tibetan Buddhist higher learning college that serves practitioners from 300 monastic traditions. Approximately 500 student-monks enroll in comprehensive 11-year courses, and the institute boasts a grand temple capable of hosting more than 4,000 individuals.
